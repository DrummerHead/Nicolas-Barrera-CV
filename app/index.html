I’m involved in the User Experience and technical implementation (HTML, CSS, JS) of Web Applications. I help teams with performance, complex layouts that must render in all viewport sizes, refactoring, usability and improving those little details that can make or break a user experience.

I try to understand the product, what value the team is adding and what the people who use the application are looking for. My ultimate goal is to align the objectives of the product with the needs of the users, and to create an intuitive and useful experience.

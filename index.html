All of the above are keywords. Standardized keywords that are useful for you to quickly grasp what is that I do. But where does Usability stop and User Experience begin? Where does wireframing stop and Information Architecture begin? For me, there is no clear line. It is the amalgamation of all of this knowledge what’s necessary for the development of the proper product.

My ultimate goal is to align the objectives of the product with the needs of the users. The best tools for this is good criterion, intuition and the flexibility to adapt to any new scenarios or tools necessary.
